ABOUT US

Hello, my name is Leon Krugliakov and I am the creator of Turakov. The idea behind it was a rather simple one. Give readers a platform where they can read hundreds of news articles from many different sources so that no one news provider can shade the truth from the reader. For example, if CNN said something that happened and then the Wall Street Journal said it as well, that means that it is more likely then not that the situation at question indeed did happen. The modern media has a tendency to blend their opinion and ideologies with the facts which is something that I personally think is wrong. Therefore I created Turakov! Which is my personal algorithm that scrapes the web for news and displays you different articles from different sources. If you like reading news and knowing what is happening in the world then Turakov is the perfect place for you!
